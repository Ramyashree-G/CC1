Our Services

Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive academic curriculum that includes Science, Mathematics, Humanities, and Languages.

Extracurricular Activities

From sports to music, drama to debate, our extracurricular activities are designed to help students explore their interests and develop new skills.

Library Services

Our well-equipped library offers a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support students in their studies and research.

Counseling Services

We provide professional counseling services to help students with academic guidance, career planning, and personal development.

Health and Wellness

Our school promotes a healthy lifestyle through physical education classes, health workshops, and access to a well-equipped clinic.

Transportation Services

We offer safe and reliable transportation services for students living in various parts of the city, ensuring they arrive at school and home safely.
